account verification


Please read these instructions carefully to be prepared for the following KYC/AML process.

For the success of the KYC process it is very important to upload high quality images. it is the best to use a high-resolution camera to take the pictures of your documents or to scan them.

Fill in all required fields on the following site.

Upload the full cover of the passport or ID card (nothing covered).When you uploaded ID card at step 2: Upload the full back of the ID card (nothing covered).

Write on a piece of paper:

"Trade Day"

current date

Take a selfie with the piece of paper and upload it.

Note: Do not wear glasses, headphones or any other accessory when taking the selfie.

Upload a document like statement of account, electricity bill or any other bill that is evidence of your current residence.Please take a close look at the criteria that the document must fulfill:

complete address

same full name in all provided documents

max. 3 months old

Click on save.

If you do not pass the KYC process, Please contact support@Trade Day.io
